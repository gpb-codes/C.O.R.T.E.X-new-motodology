## What is C.O.R.T.E.X.?

<p style="color: #2C2A33; line-height: 1.7;">
<b style="color: #4B167A;">C.O.R.T.E.X.</b> is a Personal Knowledge Management methodology designed to replace and improve upon PACE (Proyectos, Areas, Conexiones, Extracciones). The name evokes the cerebral cortex — the center of thought, connections, and learning.
</p>

<p style="color: #2C2A33; line-height: 1.7;">
It covers <b style="color: #4B167A;">four domains</b> across <b style="color: #8E44AD;">six pillars</b>:
</p>

### Weekly (Sunday, 30 min)

<ol style="color: #2C2A33; line-height: 1.8;">
  <li>Review projects: progress, blockers, next steps</li>
  <li>Review study: course progress, pending review</li>
  <li>Review startup: metrics, OKRs, funnel, decisions</li>
  <li>Evaluate areas: health metrics, checklist completion</li>
  <li>Review AXIS extractions from the week</li>
  <li>Update A.X.I.S. recurring context</li>
  <li>Plan next week</li>
</ol>

### Monthly (Last day, 60 min)

<ol style="color: #2C2A33; line-height: 1.8;">
  <li>Startup retrospective: OKRs, metrics, roadmap</li>
  <li>Monthly review of projects and study</li>
  <li>Deep evaluation of area health metrics</li>
  <li>Consolidate A.X.I.S. persistent memory</li>
  <li>Set next month's objectives</li>
</ol>
